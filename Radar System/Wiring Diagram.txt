
1. Components Required
  Arduino Uno
-HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor
-SG90 Servo Motor
-Jumper Wires
-Processing (Software for Visualization)


2. Wiring Diagram

Connections:
  HC-SR04 to Arduino:

-VCC → 5V
-GND → GND
-Trig → Pin 9
-Echo → Pin 10

  SG90 Servo to Arduino:

-VCC (Red) → 5V
-GND (Brown) → GND
-Signal (Orange) → Pin 6
